A LITTLE ABOUT ME:

I’m Hannah, an artist located in Vero Beach, Florida, on a mission to share my love of art with everyone around me! I’ve loved creating since early childhood. I’d spend hours crafting and building the perfect fort in the woods, painting details on my nails and daydreaming of the next project I’d tackle in art class.

I wanted to go to college for art, but who ever got rich from selling art? ;) So, I decided to head in a different direction - the corporate world. Years later, creating these pieces is still my happiest place in between the chaos of life.

It’s scary to showcase this to the world, but I am thrilled to be able to share my love with you. I hope it brightens your home as much as creating it has brightened my soul!
